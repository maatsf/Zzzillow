How does it work?

Having a pillow that allows you to control your dreams sounds magical and unrealistic. But there is science behind it. Studies have shown that there is a specific brain activity that happens in the frontal lobes. This is called the gamma activity . This activity is present during Lucid Dreams, and is also associated with consciousness.

A well-documented method called Transcranial Alternating Current Stimulation (tACS) helps with enhancing the gamma activity in the brain during sleep.
